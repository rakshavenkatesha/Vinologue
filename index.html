History and Origin

Wine, an ancient beverage, has its roots around 6000 BC in the Caucasus region, where natural fermentation of wild grapes first occurred. Esteemed by civilizations like the Greeks and Romans for its ceremonial significance, wine has evolved significantly. Today, it is not only celebrated for its rich heritage but also for the sophisticated art of wine quality assessment, reflecting centuries of viticultural finesse and cultural reverence.
